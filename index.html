Built by Anglers, For Anglers

FinBlade was born on the deck of a charter boat in the Florida Keys. Our founders spent decades guiding saltwater expeditions, battling everything from snook to marlin with knives that constantly failed—rusting, chipping, dulling when they mattered most.

We partnered with aerospace engineers and metallurgists to create what didn't exist: a fishing knife that could withstand the brutal reality of saltwater fishing while maintaining razor-sharp precision expedition after expedition.

Every FinBlade is tested in real conditions by working guides and tournament anglers. We don't just sell knives—we equip fellow anglers with tools we trust with our own livelihoods.
